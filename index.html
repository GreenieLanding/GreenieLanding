Importance of "Greenifying"

With more than 4.7 billion people using the internet, the carbon emissions generated by this industry has skyrocketed over the past two decades. Estimates show that carbon emissions directly related to the internet, also known as digital carbon emissions, are roughly equal to the CO2 emitted by the entire aviation industry. With more people coming online in the next decade and mass digital adoption by companies, there is a pressing need for a digital soultion that can mitigate the effects of this pending surge of carbon emissions.
